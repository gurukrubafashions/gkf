Somavara Pooja

Somavara (Monday) is auspicious day for Shiva pooja. Every Monday, we perform Rudra Abhishekam followed by Arati and Prasdam.

Pradosham Pooja

Pradhosha pooja is one of the most important poojas performed to the Graceful Lord Shiva. Pradosha Pooja with Rudrabhishekam and Shiva Ashtothra Archana is held every Pradosham day from 6:30 pm - 7:30 pm followed by Arati and Prasadam.

Pournami Pooja

Pournami - the day of full moon - is sacred for Hindus. On the evening of Pournami, poojas are performed to Satyanarayana and Arunachala. Pooja will commence at 5.30 pm. Vinayaka Pooja will be followed by Satyanarayana Pooja and Arunachala pradakshinam(Giri Valam) which is done here at Sanatan temple as circumbulation of the temple with main deity Shiva. Followed by Arati and Prasadam.

Sankatahara Chaturthi Pooja

Sankashti Chaturthi vrat and puja are described in the Bavishyat Purana and Narasimha Purana. Lord Krishna narrated the efficacy of Sankashti Chaturthi to Yudhishtira. Pooja is performed with Ganapathi Homam, Abhishekam, Ayappa Padi paatu & deepam followed by Arati and Prasadam.

Shukla Sashti Pooja

Shukla Shashti is the name for the 6th Tithi (lunar day). It's the 6th Tithi of Shuklapaksha. Lord Karttikeya (Lord of valor) presides over this day. Pooja is performed with abhishekam and alankaram to Lord Muruga followed by Arati and Prasadam.

Anusham Pooja

Anusham is the birth star of Kanchi Mahaperiyava. Rudra Abhisheka is performed to Mahaperiyava paduka and vigraham followed by Thotaka ashtakam, Bhajan, Arati and Prasadam.
